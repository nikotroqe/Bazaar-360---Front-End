About

Experience culture through immersive digital exploration.

Bazaar360 is a next-generation digital platform that brings the spirit, culture, and everyday life of Pazari i Ri into an immersive online experience.

By combining 360° virtual environments, augmented reality storytelling, and interactive navigation, Bazaar360 allows users to explore the market beyond physical boundaries — anytime, anywhere.

Our Vision

Our vision is to redefine how cultural spaces are experienced in the digital age. Bazaar360 aims to transform traditional marketplaces into living digital destinations, where stories, people, and products are seamlessly connected through technology.

What Makes Bazaar360 Unique

  • Immersive 360° exploration of real locations
  • Augmented reality stories from artisans and vendors
  • Interactive maps for intuitive navigation
  • Digital visibility for local businesses and crafts
  • Designed for both locals and international visitors

Cultural Impact

Pazari i Ri is more than a marketplace — it is a cultural meeting point. Bazaar360 preserves this identity by documenting traditions, craftsmanship, and daily interactions, ensuring they remain accessible for future generations in a modern, digital format.

Looking Ahead

Bazaar360 is built as a scalable platform, with the potential to expand into additional cultural locations, support multilingual experiences, and integrate future technologies such as advanced AR and virtual reality.
